What’s all this About

Over promising and under delivering is what drove us mad the last year.

The Wizards and Dragons game went completely the wrong way.
Not only did the market tank, but the game under delivered a lot, too.

How did we get here?
This project has been kick started out of the w&d discord. We started by collecting a couple of screenshots on stuck.lightningsoul.com to help players out that were stuck. To be more precise: People that had NFTs stuck in between the Rift. We quickly realised that we wanted to do more and have more influence on the crowdfunded game that went completely bonkers on us.
After six months of waiting we not only did not receive a game, we got a pretty buggy excuse for even more accumulation phase (now it is items that are useless). As a matter of fact 90% of w&d NFTs can not be used right now (runes (some people bought for 5 ETH), whips (some people farmed for 5 ETH gas fees in Tower II), meat etc.
So with all of this said, it's easy to understand why the project: W&D DAO was born.

Btw. we do not want any more of your money. Thought you might wanna know. SO DO NOT CLICK ANY LINKS ASKING FOR MONEY OR ANYTHING ELSE
